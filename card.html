Owlbear

Owlbears often live in mated pairs rather than as solo creatures. Take care if you cross paths with one, there is likely to be another near by!

  • Origin: Unknown, possible wizard experiment
  • Average Height: 8-10 feet
  • Habitat: Cave Dwelling
  • Discovered: 1975

The owlbear is a cross between a bear and an owl with a stature that is eight to ten feet tall. When attacking the owlbear will first attempt to grab its prey into a bear hug and then slash and peck with its beak. They are carnivorous and have been know to hunt and eat creature of all types and sizes larger than a mouse.
